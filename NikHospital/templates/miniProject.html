Obliaue National Hospital is a 750-bed, regional hospital located in London, Kentucky. Founded in 1926, Obliaue National Hospital opened a new $152 million, 340,000-square foot regional facility in 2010. We offer the latest technology along with nationally ranked, award-winning services. Our patient rooms are private with most overlooking a small lake and garden on the 52-acre healing environment.
